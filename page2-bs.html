Stress Calculator

The Perceived Stress Scale (PSS) is a classic stress assessment instrument which measures personal stress and helps us understand how different types of situations affect our emotion. The following questions, adapted from the State of New Hampshire, EAP, will ask about your feelings and thoughts during your last month. The number you receive from this calculator is your PSS.

How to use the Stress Calculator

Answer all the questions below and submit your answer. It will then return your PSS value, and will determine whether if you are stressed or not. The best approacj to the PSS assessment is to answer the questions relatively quickly and treat each question separately.
